About Our University

Chalkboard University's aim is to train students to be innovative leaders in a varied world by making it more egalitarian and inclusive. This is accomplished through recognizing each student's potential and providing opportunity for them to realize it. We assist students in determining their intended future routes, which are in service to how they define their community or societies. We educate undergraduate and graduate students for successful careers via challenging academics and assistance. We approach liberal arts and professional education in an aspirational yet practical manner, involving students in study, knowledge production, and cocurricular activities that broaden their minds while providing them with concrete tools to thrive.

Our Philosophy

Our teaching view is that all students are unique and require a challenging educational environment in which they may grow physically, cognitively, emotionally, and socially. It is our objective to provide an environment in which students may reach their greatest potential. I will provide a safe environment in which they may express their ideas and take chances.

Academics Principle

Our academic principles consist of mastery of discipline/field of study, skills in research and enquiry, personal effectiveness and self-awareness, global engagement and multicultural awareness.
